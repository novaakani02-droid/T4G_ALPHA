TEAM ALPHA

HACSA is helping young girls build valuable technology skills by creating opportunities to learn in a supportive and encouraging environment. Through practical training, mentorship, and hands-on projects, girls gain knowledge in areas such as coding, digital literacy, and other tech-related skills. This empowers them with the confidence and abilities they need to pursue careers in technology and become future innovators and leaders in their communities.

Tech gives girls the power to turn ideas into impact through creativity, leadership, and innovation.
Investing in girls in tech is investing in a brighter future for everyone.
